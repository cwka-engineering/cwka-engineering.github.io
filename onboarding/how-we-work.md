# How We Work

Interactive map of department responsibilities and collaboration across all ten CWK/DFW departments: Sales, Preconstruction, Project Management, Engineering, Purchasing, Production, Shipping/Receiving, Finance, Human Resources, and Executive.

Click any node to see that department's full responsibility list and top collaboration partners. The **E** node carries a role indicator (small dot) — its panel includes a section on transient designations (Project Advisor, Lead Engineer, Engineering Assistant). The **PUR** and **PROD** nodes note their predecessor names for reference.

> **Deep links** — append a department code as a URL anchor to open directly: `#E`, `#PM`, `#PUR`, `#PROD`, etc.
