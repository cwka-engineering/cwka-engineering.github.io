# Construction Standards

Fabrication-level construction specifications: joinery, machining, dimensions, and hardware rules for CWK/DFW product lines. This section documents *how things are built*, complementing the AWI/ANSI [Industry References](/reference/industry-references.html) (which cover *what quality grade* the result must meet).

## Available Standards

- **[Casework](/reference/construction-standards/casework/)** — Standard cabinet, drawer, filler, and hardware construction rules (carcase assembly, 32mm system, CNC boring, type codes)

> More construction standard families (countertops, hardware specs, finish specs) may be added here as they're documented.
